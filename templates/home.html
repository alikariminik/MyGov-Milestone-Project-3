Welcome

Thank you for visiting MyGovernment! If you are like me and are trying to keep up to date with what feels like a rotating door of a government, then you are in the right place. Since 2016, there have been 5 different Prime Minister's and each one of them have overseen complete shuffles of their cabinet: resignations, re-appointments and sackings of dozens of Cabinet Ministers whilst in office.

That is why I have decided to create MyGovernment - an easy way to keep my dedicated database of the current United Kingdom's Government up-to-date. This allows me to keep track of all the comings and goings at Number 10.

Want to help?

With all the changes over the last few months, we are looking for more people who believe in the importance of accountability in politics to help us maintain this site.

By creating an account with us, you will be able to make changes to the database where all the data is kept. Specifcally, by being a registered user of MyGovernment, you will be able to add new Cabinet Ministers, edit the details of exisitng ones and even delete there records for good if they were to stop being a Cabinet Minister.
